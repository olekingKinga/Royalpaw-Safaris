Kenya Highlight Safari.

Explore East Africa's beautiful wildlife and discover the magic of the animal kingdom.

Day 01: Nairobi - Kenya.

Arrive Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and after clearing with immigration proceed to arrival area where you will be met by our representative and transfer to your hotel.

Accommodation: Eka Hotel.

Meal Plan: Bed and Breakfast.

Activity: Arrival. Meet and greet. Transfer to your hotel.

Day 02: Nairobi / Nakuru National Park.

Breakfast at your hotel. After a safari briefing drive to Nakuru National Park with a stopover at Great Rift Valley for a view. Proceed to Nakuru arrive for lunch at your lodge. Afternoon have a game viewing drive in the park. The park is known well for lesser Flamingoes and endangered White rhino.

Accommodation: Lake Nakuru Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Nakuru National Park. Game drive viewing.

Day 03: Lake Nakuru National Park / Masai Mara Game Reserve.

Breakfast at your lodge. Depart and drive further to southwest and to Masai Mara Game reserve arrive for lunch at your lodge. Early evening has a game viewing in the reserve till sunsets.

Accommodation: Zebra Plains Mara Camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Game drive viewing till midnight.

Day 04: Masai Mara Game Reserve.

Spend a full day in the reserve with morning and afternoon game viewing drive or your can arrange with your professional driver guide to have a full day game with packed lunch. Today you will have a visit to Masai Village visit for a cultural experience.

Accommodation: Zebra Plains Mara Camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Game drive viewing till midnight.

Day 05: Masai Mara Game Reserve / Nairobi/Depart.

Breakfast at your lodge. Depart and drive to Nairobi arriving for lunch at Carnivore restaurant. Later transfer to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for your departure flight.
