12 Days / 11 Nights: Kenya / Tanzania Safari 2026.

Explore East Africa's beautiful wildlife and discover the magic of the animal kingdom.

Day 01: Nairobi- Kenya.

Arrive Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and after clearing with immigration proceed to arrival area where you will be met by our representative and transfer to your hotel.

Accommodation: Eka Hotel.

Meal Plan: Bed and Breakfast.

Activity: Arrival. Meet and greet. Transfer to your hotel.

Day 02: Lake Nakuru National Park / Masai Mara Game Reserve.

Breakfast at your hotel. Depart and drive further to southwest and to Masai Mara Game reserve arrive for lunch at your lodge. Early evening has a game viewing in the reserve till sunsets.

Accommodation: Zebra Plains Mara Camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Transfer to Masai Mara Game Reserve. Game drive viewing.

Day 03 & Day 04: Masai Mara Game Reserve.

Spend another two full days in the reserve with morning and afternoon game viewing drive or your can arrange with your professional driver guide to have a full day game with packed lunch. Today you will have a visit to Masai Village visit for a cultural experience.

Accommodation: Zebra Plains Mara camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Game drive viewing in Masai Mara Game Reserve.

Day 05: Masai Mara Game Reserve / Serengeti National park.

Breakfast at your camp. Drive to Kenya / Tanzania border where after clearing with immigration proceed to Tanzania side where you will join our professional driver guide and drive to Serengeti Central with an enroute game viewing. Dinner and overnight.

Accommodation: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Drive to Serengeti. Afternoon game drive viewing.

Day 06 & 07: Serengeti National Park.

Spend another two full days in the famous Serengeti National Park for game viewing drive. You will arrange with our professional driver guide to have full days with lunch boxes to explore the park to full end. The vast Serengeti plains cover an area of over 14,763 square kilometers and during the months of the migration, is the habitat of huge numbers of wildebeest, zebra and Thomson’s gazelle with estimates of up to 1.5 million.

The Serengeti “Kopjes”, massive boulder islands of granite standing in a sea of grass, provide shelter to a great variety of plants and animals, from the elusive leopard to the tiny dik-dik.

Accommodation: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Full day game drive viewing.

Day 08: Serengeti National Park /Ngorongoro conservation area.

Breakfast at the lodge. Check out with an enroute game drive as you depart Serengeti. Continue to Ngorongoro Crater Conservation Area (NCCA), which features rolling plains, lakes, craters, mountains and dormant volcanoes. Afternoon rlax at your lodge and enjoy facilities offered by the lodge. Dinner and Overnight at your lodge. relaxation before dinner.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Serena safari Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Ngorongoro crater.

Day 09: Crater.

After breakfast at your lodge. Descend into the ‘8th Wonder of the World’, a unique biosphere that has remained virtually unchanged since the dawn of time.

Enclosed within its towering walls are grasslands, swamps, forests, saltpans, a fresh water lake and a glorious variety of birdlife. Here too are all the members of the ‘Big Five’ (lion, elephant, rhino, leopard, and buffalo) as well as a large cast of plains game and one of Africa’s densest populations of predators.

Enjoy a picnic lunch on the crater floor before driving back to your lodge for relaxation before dinner.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, packed lunch and dinner .

Activity: Game drive.

Day 10: Ngorongoro Conservation area / Amboseli National Park.

Breakfast at your lodge. Depart and drive to Arusha arrive for early lunch.

Later proceed to Namanga border where after clearing with immigration department proceed to Kenyan side where you will have received by your professional driver guide and proceed to Amboseli National Park arrive at your camp in the late afternoon. Evening relax at your camp.

Accommodation: Kibo Tented Camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Game drive viewing.

Day 11: Amboseli National Park.

The day starts with an early morning game viewing where you’ll see white rhinos, big tusked elephants, big cats, wildebeests, zebras and many other species. If the skies are clear you will have clear views of the snowcapped Mt. Kilimanjaro. Amazingly, the sites you see in the morning will be quite different from those you’ll see on the afternoon game drive as the various creatures respond to their unique biological clocks.

Depending on the time of year, the terrain will vary from snows of Kilimanjaro to the hot dry summer to the life-giving rainy season – but it will always be beautiful. The routes taken will depend on weather conditions but they are all offer unforgettable sceneries, so keep your camera ready. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Accommodation: Kibo Safari Camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Morning and afternoon game viewing drives.

Day 12: Amboseli National Park / Nairobi/Depart.

Breakfast at your lodge. Later drive to Nairobi arrive to Carnivore restaurant for lunch. Later in the afternoon transfer to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for your departure flight

Accommodation: Kibo Safari Camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast and lunch.

Activity: Road transfer to Nairobi. Drop off at JKIA for departure flight.
