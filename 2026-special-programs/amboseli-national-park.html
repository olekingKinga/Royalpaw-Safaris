Two Nights Amboseli National Park.

Explore East Africa's beautiful wildlife and discover the magic of the animal kingdom.

Day 01: Tanzania/ Kenya- Amboseli National Park.

After clearing with immigration at the border of Namanga proceed to parking area where you will meet Kenyan professional English speaking driver guide and drive to Amboseli National Park arriving with en route game viewing and to your camp.

Accommodation: Kibo Safari Tented Camp.

Meal Plan: Dinner and Breakfast.

Activity: Arrival. Meet and greet. Transfer to your hotel.

Day 02: Amboseli National Park.

Spend another full day with morning and afternoon game viewing drive. This park is known for high concentration of African Elephant and also face of Mount Kilimanjaro the highest free standing mountain in the world.

Accommodation: Kibo Safari Tented Camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Transfer to Amboseli National Park. Game drive viewing.

Day 03: Amboseli National Park / Nairobi / Depart.

Breakfast at your camp. Late drive to Nairobi arriving for lunch at Carnivore restaurant. Later transfer to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for your departure flight.
