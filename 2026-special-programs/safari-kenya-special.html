10 days /09 Nights Safari - Kenya Special.

Explore East Africa's beautiful wildlife and discover the magic of the animal kingdom.

Day 01: Nairobi- Kenya.

Arrive Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and after clearing with immigration proceed to arrival area where you will be met by our representative and transfer to your hotel.

Accommodation: Eka Hotel.

Meal Plan: Bed and Breakfast.

Activity: Arrival. Meet and greet. Transfer to your hotel.

Day 02: Nairobi / Ol Pajeta Conservancy.

Breakfast at your hotel. After a safari briefing board your vehicle and drive to Central highlands and to northern side of Kenya. Arrive at the conservancy in time for lunch. Afternoon have a game viewing drive in the conservancy.

Accommodation: Maisha Sweetwaters Camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Transfer to Ol Pajeta Conservancy. Game drive viewing.

Day 03: Ol pajeta Conservancy.

Spend a full day with morning and afternoon game viewing drive in the conservancy. Please note conservancy boast the largest black rhino sanctuary in East Africa. You will enjoy African apes among others.

Accommodation: Maisha Sweetwaters Camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Transfer to Ol Pajeta Conservancy. Game drive viewing.

Day 04: Ol Pajeta Conservancy / Samburu National Reserve.

Breakfast at your camp. Depart and drive further to northern Kenya and to Samburu National reserve arrive for lunch at your lodge. After siesta have evening game viewing game.

Accommodation: Samburu Sopa Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Transfer to Samburu National Reserve. Game drive viewing.

Day 05: Samburu National Reserve.

As it is known for unique animals only found north of Equator such as Grey zebra, Gerneruk antelope, Somali Ostrich among others, it good to have this stay with game viewing.

Accommodation: Samburu Sopa Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Game drive viewing in Samburu National Reserve.

Day 06: Samburu National Reserve / Nakuru National Park.

Breakfast at your lodge. Drive to Nakuru National Park with a stopover at Nyahururu falls for a view. Proceed to Nakuru arrive for lunch at your lodge. Afternoon have a game viewing drive in the park. The park is know well for lesser Flamingoes and endangered White rhino.

Accommodation: Lake Nakuru Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Transfer to Nakuru National Park. Game drive viewing.

Day 07: Lake Nakuru National Park / Masai Mara Game Reserve.

Breakfast at your lodge. Depart and drive further to southwest and to Masai Mara Game reserve arrive for lunch at your lodge. Early evening have a game viewing in the reserve till sunsets.

Accommodation: Zebra Plains Mara Camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Transfer to Masai Mara Game Reserve. Game drive viewing.

Day 08 & 09: Masai Mara Game Reserve.

Spend two full days in the reserve with morning and afternoon game viewing drive or your can arrange with your professional driver guide to have a full day game with packed lunch. Today you will have a visit to Masai Village visit for a cultural experience.

Accommodation: Zebra Plains Mara Camp

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Game drive viewing.

Day 10: Masai Mara Game Reserve / Nairobi/Depart.

Breakfast at your lodge. Depart and drive to Nairobi arriving for lunch at Carnivore restaurant. Later transfer to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for your departure flight.
