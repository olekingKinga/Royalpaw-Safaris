Tanzania/Kenya Best Safari-KE-TZ-003.

Explore East Africa's beautiful wildlife and discover the magic of the animal kingdom.

Day 01- Tanzania- Arusha.

Arrive JRO International Airport and after clearing with immigrations proceed to the arrival hall where you will be met by our representative and transfer to Kibo Palace Hotel.

Accommodation: Kibo Palace Hotel.

Meal Plan: Bed and breakfast.

Activity: Arrival. Meet and greet. Transfer to your hotel.

Day 2: Nairobi Excursion

Breakfast at your hotel. Met with our representative for a safari briefing and later drive to Manyara National Park for game viewing. Later depart the park and to your camp for lunch. Afternoon you will enjoy hotel facilities offered.

Accommodation: Manyara Serena safari Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Transfer to Lake Manyara National Park. Game drive viewing.

Day 03: Lake Manyara National Park / Ngorongoro Conservation area.

Breakfast at your hotel. Depart and drive to Ngorongoro conservation area and descend to the crater for game viewing drive. Later ascend to the rim for your lunch. Afternoon enjoy hotel facilities offered.

Accommodation: Samburu Sopa.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Road transfer to Samburu. Afternoon game viewing drive.

Day 04: Ngorongoro Conservation area /Serengeti National Park.

Breakfast at your lodge. Depart and drive to Serengeti National Park arrive with en route game viewing drive to your lodge for lunch. Afternoon relax before you have you evening game drive.

Accommodation: Serengeti Sopa Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Road transfer to Serengeti. Game drive viewing.

Day 05: Serengeti National Park- Central area.

Spend a full day in the central Serengeti with full day game or you can discuss with your guide to have morning and afternoon game with lunches at your lodge. Optional activity available at an extra cost includes Hot air balloon over Serengeti Eco-system.

Accommodation: Serengeti Sopa Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Full day game drive viewing.

Day 06: Serengeti National Park â€“ North area.

After breakfast enroute game to northern side as you search for big five and other migrators wild animals. Lunch will be at your camp. Afternoon relax and enjoy sunset at your camp.

Accommodation: Mbalageti safari camp.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Game drive viewing in Northern Serengeti.

Day 07: Serengeti National Park/ Masai Mara Game Reserve.

After breakfast at the camp. Depart with packed lunch and drive to Tanzania/Kenya border where you will be received by your English speaking professional driver guide and drive to Masai Mara. Arrive late afternoon at your lodge with en route game.

Accommodation: Mara Sopa Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Road transfer to Masai Mara.

Day 08: Masai Mara Game Reserve.

Spend a full day in the reserve with morning and afternoon game viewing drive in the reserve. On this day or the way, you can plan with your guide you will have a visit to Masai Village for a cultural experience.

Accommodation: :Mara Sopa Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Full day game viewing drive.

Day 09 : Masai Mara Game Reserve / Lake Nakuru National park.

After breakfast drive to Nakuru National Park arriving for lunch at your lodge. Afternoon have a game drive in the park where chance to see the endangered species like Black rhino is high. As Nakuru is well known for lesser and greater flamingoes you will enjoy at this moment.

Accommodation: Lake Nakuru Sopa Lodge.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Road transfer to Lake Nakuru.

Day 10: Depart

Breakfast at your lodge. Later drive to Nairobi, arriving in time for lunch at Carnivore restaurant for lunch. Later in the afternoon transfer to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for your departure flight.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Activity: Ngorongoro crater.
